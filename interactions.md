# Interactions to replicate (based on screenshots)

General
- Transitions are subtle (150–250ms). No dramatic motion.
- Hover: borders/glow slightly intensify; text stays crisp; images do not wildly zoom.

Hero
- CTA buttons: hover brightens border/glow; focus ring subtle.

Projects Mosaic / Carousel
- Each project tile has an overlay “View Casestudy” glass pill pinned near the bottom.
- Whole tile is clickable (preferred). Overlay remains clickable.
- On smaller screens, allow horizontal scroll with snap.

Process
- Step cards: slight hover lift (2–4px) + border brighten.

Services
- Chips: hover/active states brighten slightly.
- Capability tag scroller: overflow-x, hide scrollbar.

Reviews
- Review cards: subtle hover brighten.

FAQ
- Accordion expands/collapses with a short height animation.
- Open item shows a close “x” on the right (as seen), otherwise show “+” or chevron.
